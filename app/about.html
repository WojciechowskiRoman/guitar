About Us

Chill Shop today is live stores in USA, our own guitar repair shops, a design and installation department, a tender department and, of course, an online store.

We have been selling musical instruments professionally since 2005. We work not only with brands officially represented in USA, but also bring the necessary tools from the USA, Europe, Japan, Russia, China or any other country to order from the manufacturer's warehouse.

The status of an official dealer for brands such as Fender, Gibson, Ibanez, Carvin, Jackson, Mayones, Yamaha, Hohner, Ernie Ball and many others gives you access to the largest possible range and the lowest possible prices.

We are able to deliver throughout the country not only guitars and strings, but also large or fragile goods, whether it is a 100 kg tube amplifier, a grand piano or an 8 meter long projector screen.

Each Chill Shop brand store is equipped with everything necessary for comfortable testing of the instrument: soft sofas, wires, amplifiers, pedals, microphones, and more. And in stores, you can pay for goods with a card or make a purchase on credit.

The commission department always has a large selection of vintage guitars. Here you will find both Gibson of the early seventies or purebred Japanese Fernandes of the mid-nineties, as well as quite decent modern electric guitars at reasonable prices.

Every used guitar undergoes mandatory pre-sale preparation: we change the strings for new ones, adjust the bridge and adjust the truss if necessary. And if more serious repairs are required, then we carry out that too. So you can be sure - all vintage guitars in our shop not only look, but sound just great!
