  • RG3EXFM1 is a model from the RG Fixed series in the Ibanez catalog. This series, together with the RG Tremolo, forms the introductory level for the RG segment. It offers budget-friendly double-cut guitars with fixed bridges and 24 fret fingerboards. RG3EXFM1 is one such model, it features a solid body made of basswood back and flamed maple top. Top panel is bound in white and finished in high gloss. Transparent Gray Burst and Blackberry Burst are color options available for this model. Fixed bridge on this six-stringer is equipped with string-through-body construction and it`s plated in cosmo black. Pair of active Ibanez humbuckers is installed in front of the bridge. Maple neck is made of three pieces of wood and it is topped with a 24-fret rosewood fingerboard.
